TECHCORP COMPANY POLICIES
==========================

VACATION AND LEAVE POLICY
-------------------------
At TechCorp, we believe in work-life balance. Our vacation policy includes:

- All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year
- PTO accrues at 1.67 days per month
- Maximum PTO carryover to the next year is 5 days
- Sick leave is separate: 10 days per year
- Parental leave: 16 weeks for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary caregivers

REMOTE WORK POLICY
-----------------
TechCorp supports flexible work arrangements:

- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in your local timezone
- Remote work equipment stipend: $500 one-time for home office setup
- Internet reimbursement: $50/month for remote workers

EXPENSE POLICY
--------------
Business expense guidelines:

- Meals during travel: $75/day maximum
- Hotel: $200/night maximum (higher limits for SF, NYC, and other high-cost cities)
- Flights: Economy class for trips under 5 hours, Business class for longer flights
- All expenses require receipts and must be submitted within 30 days

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
------------------------
TechCorp invests in employee growth:

- Annual learning budget: $2,500 per employee
- Conference attendance: Up to 2 conferences per year
- Certification exams: 100% covered by the company
- Internal tech talks every Friday at 4 PM
