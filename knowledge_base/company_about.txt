TECHCORP MISSION, VISION, AND VALUES
=====================================

OUR MISSION
-----------
To empower businesses with intelligent technology solutions that drive 
innovation, efficiency, and growth.

OUR VISION
----------
A world where AI augments human potential, making work more meaningful 
and impactful for everyone.

CORE VALUES
-----------

1. INNOVATION FIRST
   We constantly push boundaries and embrace new ideas. Every team member
   is encouraged to experiment and learn from failures.

2. CUSTOMER OBSESSION
   Our customers' success is our success. We go above and beyond to 
   understand their needs and deliver exceptional value.

3. INTEGRITY ALWAYS
   We do the right thing, even when no one is watching. Transparency 
   and honesty guide all our decisions.

4. ONE TEAM
   We collaborate across boundaries, celebrate diverse perspectives,
   and support each other's growth.

5. SUSTAINABLE IMPACT
   We build for the long term, considering our impact on communities,
   the environment, and future generations.


COMPANY HISTORY
---------------
Founded in 2015 in San Francisco, TechCorp started as a small team of 
5 engineers with a vision to democratize AI. Today, we serve over 
10,000 customers worldwide with a team of 500+ employees across 
12 offices.

Key Milestones:
- 2015: Company founded, first product launched
- 2017: Series A funding ($15M), expanded to 50 employees
- 2019: Series B funding ($50M), opened European headquarters
- 2021: Series C funding ($150M), launched AI Assistant Pro
- 2023: Reached profitability, 10,000 customers milestone
- 2025: Preparing for IPO, global expansion
